
#  Dashboard Overview

The Spend Scope Insights Dashboard provides an executive-level view of customer transaction data, highlighting revenue flow, category performance, and customer segmentation.  
It is designed to help business stakeholders understand how money flows, where it comes from, and which customer groups drive revenue.

#  Key Business Questions Answered

- How much total revenue is generated?
- Which categories contribute most to revenue?
- How does revenue trend over time?
- Which customer segments contribute the most value?
- How does spending differ by gender?

#  KPI Summary

* Average Spend per Customer: 442.12 | Typical customer transaction value
* Total Revenue : 22.11M | Total business income across all transactions
*  Total Transactions : 50K | Overall transaction volume
*  Revenue Contribution % : 100% | Distribution across all categories 

#  Category Revenue Contribution

* Travel : 12.90M | 58%
* Electronics : 4.39M | 20%
* Market : 2.15M | 10%
* Clothing : 1.32M | 6%
* Cosmetic : 0.88M | 4%
* Restaurant : 0.46M | 2%

#  Insight
- Travel is the dominant revenue driver , contributing over half of total revenue.
- Electronics is the second strongest category.
- Restaurant and Cosmetic categories show low revenue contribution, indicating potential growth opportunities.

# Customer Segmentation Insights 

# Total Spend by Segment
- High Spenders: 11.47M (51.9%)
- Low Spenders: 6.23M (28.2%)
- Medium Spenders: 4.40M (19.9%)

# Insight
- High-value customers generate more than half of total revenue.
- Business performance is highly dependent on retaining high spenders.
- Medium and low spenders present opportunities for targeted marketing and upselling.

#  Monthly Revenue Trend

The monthly transaction trend shows relatively stable spending across the year, with periodic peaks indicating high-activity months.  
This helps management identify seasonal spending behavior and plan promotions accordingly.

#  Gender-Based Analysis

A gender slicer allows interactive filtering to observe spending patterns across male and female customers, supporting demographic-based marketing strategies.

#  Business Value Delivered

This dashboard enables management to:
- Monitor overall revenue performance
- Identify top-performing categories
- Understand customer value distribution
- Design targeted customer retention strategies
- Recognize seasonal and behavioral spending patterns
